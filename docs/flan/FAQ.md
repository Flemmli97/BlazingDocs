### How do I add someone to a claim?
> GUI (recommended): `/flan menu` -> Edit permission group -> Add the player to the desired group. You can type their name in an anvil-like interface.  
>
> Commands: group players \<add \| remove> \<name> \<players>

### Where are the configs located?
> Configs can be located in the `/config` folder of your minecraft/server files and then under the `/flan` directory

### How do I blacklist items/blocks/entities from other mods?
> The config supports ignoring them. e.g. `breakBlockBlacklist`

### How do I get more claim blocks?
> You gain claimblocks with playtime.

### How do I increase the default/max number of claim blocks?
> You can adjust the claim block settings in the config file. There are options to set the default number of claim blocks and how they increase over time.

### How do I disable mob/fall/other damage in admin claims?
> Currently, there is no built-in feature to disable fall damage in claims. A workaround is giving players in claims resistance 5

### A block/item/entity is assigned a permission I don't like/feel like doesn't fit. How do I change it?
> You can change the assigned permission for that. For that out both [Custom Permission](/dev/Custom%20Permission.md) and [Interaction Overrides](/dev/Interaction%20Overrides.md) pages.
