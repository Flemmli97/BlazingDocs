## FLAN

Welcome to the wiki of FLAN.

FLAN is a completely server side fabric & (neo)forge mod that adds claims to your world.

If you are a player head to the [Getting Started](/user_guides/Getting-Started.md) section

Most of the content in FLAN is editable via server sided guis but everything is also possible through the use of commands only.
